1. Exactly 6 panels in this order: Motivation, Problem, Method, Experiments, Results, Conclusion.
2. Each panel: 3–5 bullets, each ≤ 140 characters, declarative and paper-grounded

3. Use figures sparingly: at most 2 panels carry a figure (typically Method and
   Results). For the others, leave figure_id empty and layout_hint = "text_only".
4. figure_id must match one of the figure ids provided in the FIGURE LIST below.
5. Do NOT invent numbers, citations, or claims that the paper does not state.
6. Output a single JSON object. No preamble, no trailing text, no Markdown fences.
